Toy Blast
---------
* 9x9
* one click matches non-diagonal connected dots
* bubble=cage with item, brick=door without item, balloon=blocker, 
* rewards: 5=blaster (rocket), 7=bomb (dynamite), 9=ColorBomb (globe)
* combos: 2 striped=

Jelly Splash
------------
* 7x9
* diagonal
* min chain length = 3
* rewards: wrapped dots, random placement, sometimes more than one - not sure if it depends on chain length...
* blaster (wrapped dot) activates at end of chain
* blasters combine, when two blasters hit each other, all blasters go both hor./vertically
* slippery anchors
